About

This is the website of a fictional restaurant. "Sergiosja" is a russian version of my first name, Sergey. "de la" roughly translates to "of the" in French. "Res" is an abbreviation of the english word "Restaurant". As you can see, the title is made up of three languages, and makes no sense.

I created the webside because I wanted to implement a cinema-like feature you can check out if you attempt to reserve a table. I have never seen it on any other restaurant website I have visited in the past. Perhaps it is unnecessary, but I personally would not mind to know where I will be seated during a restaurant visit. Especially if you are to be seated for hours. Do you want to be near the bar? Near the bathrooms? As far away from the entrance as possible?
